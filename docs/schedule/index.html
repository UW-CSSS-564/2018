Schedule

This schedule may be altered as the needs of the course. Students will be informed of changes to the schedule.

Subject to time constraints, I expect to cover the following topics.

  • Bayes Theorem
  • Conjugate posterior distributions
  • Bayesian Inference
  • Bayesian computational methods
  • Introduction to Stan
  • Regression Models
  • Diagnosing sampling issues
  • Comparing models
  • Shrinkage and sparsity in Bayesian Models
  • Hierarchical Models
  • Measurement models
