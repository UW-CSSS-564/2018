### Decision Theory

- A **decision rule** is a rule that maps an observation to an action.
- It requires a **loss** function for the outcome of each action under different states.
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayes_estimator
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Admissible_decision_rule
